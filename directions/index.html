Venue

The Bootcamp takes place at the Unipark Nonntal at University Salzburg. You find the venue at Erzabt-Klotz-Straße 1, 5020 Salzburg. Here is the local map.

How to reach the venue?

From the train station:

If you travel to Salzburg by train, you will arrive at Salzburg central station (Hauptbahnhof: Südtiroler Platz 1, 5020 Salzburg). From the central station, you can take different buses to the venue. Here are some possibilities:

  • Take the Obus 5 (direction: Birkensiedlung) and get off at the stop Salzburg Hofwirt. Walk for about 5 minutes to the venue.
  • Take the Obus 3 (direction: Salzburg Landstraße) until the stop Salzburg Kirchenstraße and walk in 6 min to the venue.

From the airport:

Travelling to Salzburg by plane will lead you to Salzburg Airport (SZG) located at Innsbrucker Bundesstraße 95, 5020 Salzburg. There are different possibilities to take local transport from the airport to the venue starting from the bus station Salzburg Airport Terminal:

  • Take the Obus 2 (direction: Hauptbahnhof-Obergnigl) to Salzburg Hauptbahnhof and transfer to the Obus 5 (direction: Birkensiedlung). Get off at the stop Salzburg Hofwirt and walk for about 5 minutes to the venue.
  • Take the Regionalbus 180 (direction: Salzburg Hauptbahnhof) to Salzburg Hauptbahnhof and transfer to the Obus 3 (direction: Salzburg Alpensiedlung Süd). Get off at the stop Salzburg Mirabellplatz and walk for about 6 minutes to the venue.

General information for local transports:

You can find schedules and further information for local transports, as well as prices and tickets on the following link: https://salzburg-verkehr.at/
