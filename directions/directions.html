Venue

Information about the bootcamp location

The 2nd Reinforcement Learning Bootcamp will take place at the Unipark Nonntal, a modern facility of the University of Salzburg.

Address:
Unipark Nonntal
Erzabt-Klotz-Straße 1
5020 Salzburg, Austria

Venue Facilities

The Unipark Nonntal offers modern facilities including:

  • Auditorium for keynote presentations
  • Multiple seminar rooms for workshops
  • Computer labs for hands-on sessions
  • Cafeteria and dining options
  • Free Wi-Fi throughout the building
  • Accessibility features for all attendees

How to Reach the Venue

Transportation options to get to the bootcamp

From the Train Station

If you travel to Salzburg by train, you will arrive at Salzburg central station (Hauptbahnhof: Südtiroler Platz 1, 5020 Salzburg). From the central station, you can take different buses to the venue:

  • Obus 5 (direction: Birkensiedlung) → Get off at the stop Salzburg Hofwirt → Walk for about 5 minutes to the venue.
  • Obus 3 (direction: Salzburg Landstraße) → Get off at the stop Salzburg Kirchenstraße → Walk for about 6 minutes to the venue.

From the Airport

Travelling to Salzburg by plane will lead you to Salzburg Airport (SZG) located at Innsbrucker Bundesstraße 95, 5020 Salzburg. From the bus station Salzburg Airport Terminal, you can reach the venue by:

  • Obus 2 (direction: Hauptbahnhof-Obergnigl) → To Salzburg Hauptbahnhof → Transfer to Obus 5 (direction: Birkensiedlung) → Get off at Salzburg Hofwirt → Walk for about 5 minutes to the venue.
  • Regionalbus 180 (direction: Salzburg Hauptbahnhof) → To Salzburg Hauptbahnhof → Transfer to Obus 3 (direction: Salzburg Alpensiedlung Süd) → Get off at Salzburg Mirabellplatz → Walk for about 6 minutes to the venue.
