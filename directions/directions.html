Venue

Information about the bootcamp location

The 2nd Reinforcement Learning Bootcamp will take place at the Unipark Nonntal, a modern facility of the University of Salzburg.

Address:
Lecture Hall: Agnes Muthspiel
Unipark Nonntal
Erzabt-Klotz-Straße 1
5020 Salzburg, Austria

Getting to Unipark Nonntal

Directions from Salzburg Hauptbahnhof and Salzburg Airport

From Salzburg Hauptbahnhof (Central Station)

Option 1 – Obus Line 3 (Trolleybus)
Board: Obus 3 at Salzburg Hauptbahnhof
Direction: Salzburg Süd
Get off: Justizgebäude stop
Walk: ~5 minutes to Unipark Nonntal

Option 2 – Obus Line 5 (Trolleybus)
Board: Obus 5 at Salzburg Hauptbahnhof
Direction: Birkensiedlung
Get off: Justizgebäude stop
Walk: ~5 minutes to Unipark Nonntal

From Salzburg Airport (SZG)

Option 1 – Obus Line 10
Board: Obus 10 at Salzburg Airport Terminal
Direction: Sam
Get off: Salzburg Justizgebäude
Walk: ~6 minutes to Unipark Nonntal

Option 2 – Obus Line 2,8
Board: Obus 2 at Salzburg Airport Terminal
Direction: Obergnigl
Get off: Aigholf LKH West(R.-Biebl-Str)
Transfer: Walk to Aigholf LKH West(Aigholfstr) and board Obus 8
Get off: Salzburg Justizgebäude
Walk: ~7 minutes to Unipark Nonntal

Where to Stay

Recommended hotels near Unipark Nonntal and in Salzburg's historic center

Old Town Hotels (Altstadt)

  • Altstadthotel Käserbräu — Kaigasse 33
  • Altstadthotel Weiße Taube — Kaigasse 9

Great for experiencing Salzburg’s historic center, 10 min. walk to the venue.

Hotels Near Unipark Nonntal

  • Hotel Via Roma — Nonntaler Hauptstraße 47
  • JUFA Hotel Salzburg — Josef-Preis-Allee 18

These hotels are within walking distance (~5–10 min) of the venue.

Additional Options

  • Hotel zum Hirschen — Elisabethstraße 5
  • Wyndham Grand Salzburg Conference Centre — Fanny-von-Lehnert-Straße 7
  • MEININGER Hotel Salzburg — Fürbergstraße 18-20

Well-connected by bus or taxi; good options for different budgets and comfort levels.
