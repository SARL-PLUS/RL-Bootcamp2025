Registration Overview

Everything you need to know about registering for the 2nd RL Bootcamp

The 2nd Reinforcement Learning Bootcamp is open to students, researchers, and professionals interested in reinforcement learning. Registration is completely free of charge thanks to our sponsors, but spaces are limited. Please register early to secure your spot!

Important Dates

Registration opens: April 15, 2025
Registration deadline: August 15, 2025
Bootcamp dates: September 17-19, 2025

Who Can Attend

The bootcamp is designed for participants with basic knowledge of machine learning concepts. No prior experience with reinforcement learning is required.

What to Bring

Participants should bring their own laptop with Python installed. Pre-installation instructions will be sent after registration is confirmed.

Certificate

All participants will receive a certificate of completion after attending the bootcamp.
