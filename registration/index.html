Frequently Asked Questions

Who can attend the bootcamp?

The bootcamp is open to anyone interested in reinforcement learning, including students, researchers, and industry professionals. Basic knowledge of machine learning concepts and Python programming is recommended.

Do I need to bring my own laptop?

Yes, you should bring your own laptop for the hands-on workshop sessions. Setup instructions will be sent to registered participants prior to the event.

Will certificates be provided?

Yes, all participants who attend the complete bootcamp will receive a certificate of participation.

Can I cancel my registration?

Yes, you can cancel your registration at any time. However, we kindly ask you to inform us as soon as possible so we can offer your spot to someone else.

Is accommodation included?

No, participants are responsible for arranging their own accommodation. Please see our Accommodation page for recommended hotels near the venue.

How can I contact the organizers?

For any questions related to registration or the bootcamp in general, please contact: rl-bootcamp@plus.ac.at
